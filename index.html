How DAMS works

Each sensor reports to the Arduino node, data streams to the cloud, and the dashboard renders live status plus alerts. The flow below shows the full lifecycle.

1

Sense

Temperature, smoke, water, and vibration sensors capture the environment every few seconds.

2

Transmit

Arduino pushes readings to the cloud gateway with timestamps and device ID.

3

Analyze

Rules classify safe, warning, or critical states for each sensor channel.

4

Respond

Dashboards trigger banners, status pills, and alert guidance for responders.
